Panzer (C1T)
Cementitious tile adhesive for walls and ﬂoors. Indoor and outdoor

What is this product?

It's a white/grey cement-glue for fixing and bonding medium-high absortion ceramic tiles.
On the following substrates:

  • Indoor and outdoor.
  • Cement, concrete.
  • Traditional brick..

Characteristics

  • Monocomponent mortar-glue (Only needs water).
  • Slip resistant.
  • Thin coat application.
  • Excellent workability for walls and floors.
  • High frost resistance.

How to prepare?

  1. Pour the water first and then, the mortar-glue.
  2. Mix in bowls in an approximated proportion of 6 litres each 25 kgs.
  3. It is very important to not add more water than recommended, because physical and chemical conditions would be then, reduced.
  4. Mix by hand or with a mixer machine (better slow, 500 rpm) until getting a creamy paste.
  5. Let it repose 5 minutes for the additives to act correctly and then mix again before use.
  6. Never add more water after the paste settles.

How to use?

Substrates must be clean and dry.
Extend the product with the smooth part of the trowel to ensure a complete contact with the substrate.
Use the dented trowel (no. 6-8) in 1,5 - 2 m2 squares.
Check that no surfacial film is formed over the adhesive.
In such a case, it's enough to pass the trowel again with some fresh product.
Place the ceramic leaving 2-3 mm joints.

We recommend:

  • To leave 3 mm expansion joints in the extremes of the walls.
  • For floors, leave 6 mm perimeter joints.

Where to use?

Ceramic tiles fixing, for walls and floors.
Substrates: Cement, cement-lime mortar, brick and cement floors.
Substrates and surfaces must be solid, hard, clean and dry, without dust, grease or oxide.
Do not use: Over metal, concrete, fiber-cement, wood, tiles and old tiles.
In these cases, mix with ADESILATEX 1000 (6 litres each 25 kgs).

Tapazul white joint coverer
Cementitious tile grout

What is this product?

It's a paste for 1 to 15 mm joints between tiles.
Suitable for any kind of tiles refilling and sealing.
Available in white and in colours.
Special colours can be done after development.

Characteristics

  • Monocomponent (only needs water).
  • 1-15 mm joint widths.
  • Excellent workability and easy application.
  • High hardness and frost resistant.
  • Hydrofuge.

How to prepare?

  1. Pour the water first and then, the paste.
  2. Mix in bowls in an approximated proportion of 1,8 litres each 6 kgs.
  3. It's very important to not add more water than recommended, physical and chemical conditions of the product would be then, reduced.
  4. Mix by hand or with a mixer machine (better slow, 500 rpm) until getting a creamy paste.
  5. Let it repose during 5 minutes before application, in order to additives to act correctly and the, mix again before use.
  6. Never add water after the paste settles.

How to use?

Rejointment must be done after 24 hours of the ceramics fixing.
Clean all the rests of adhesive from the joints.
Mix 'Tapazul' until getting a creamy paste without lumps.
Fill the joints with product; this operation can be done with different tools: Brush, filling gun, gum spatula...
Once the joints are filled, let the product reposes until it starts getting hard, when the product rests will be cleaned with a sponge.
After 24 hours of the rejointment, the surface can be cleaned with a wet cloth.
