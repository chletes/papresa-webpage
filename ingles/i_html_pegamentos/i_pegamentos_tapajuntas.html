Tapazul (CG2)
Cementitious tile grout

What is this product?

It's a paste for 1 to 15 mm joints between tiles.
Suitable for any kind of tiles refilling and sealing.
Available in white and in colours.
Special colours can be done after development.

Characteristics

  • Monocomponent (only needs water).
  • 1-15 mm joint widths.
  • Excellent workability and easy application.
  • High hardness and frost resistant.
  • Hydrofuge.

How to prepare?

  1. Pour the water first and then, the paste.
  2. Mix in bowls in an approximated proportion of 1,8 litres each 6 kgs.
  3. It's very important to not add more water than recommended, physical and chemical conditions of the product would be then, reduced.
  4. Mix by hand or with a mixer machine (better slow, 500 rpm) until getting a creamy paste.
  5. Let it repose during 5 minutes before application, in order to additives to act correctly and the, mix again before use.
  6. Never add water after the paste settles.

How to use?

Rejointment must be done after 24 hours of the ceramics fixing.
Clean all the rests of adhesive from the joints.
Mix 'Tapazul' until getting a creamy paste without lumps.
Fill the joints with product; this operation can be done with different tools: Brush, filling gun, gum spatula...
Once the joints are filled, let the product reposes until it starts getting hard, when the product rests will be cleaned with a sponge.
After 24 hours of the rejointment, the surface can be cleaned with a wet cloth.

Pasta de Juntas
Special paste for gypsum board unions

What is this product?

This paste is specially indicated for fixation and rejointment of gypsum board in interior walls.

Characteristics

  • White powder to mix with water.
  • Excellent workability for interiors.
  • High performance.
  • Suitable thicken for gypsum board rejointment.
  • Mechanic or manual application.

How to prepare?

  1. Pour the water in a bowl and then, the product in an approximated proportion of 12 litres each 25 kgs.
  2. Mix by hand or with a mixer machine (better slow, 500 rpm) until getting a creamy paste.
  3. Let it repose 5 minutes for the additives to act correctly.
  4. Mix energically until getting a proper density.

Notes: A shaking excess might cause an forging process acceleration.
Never use more water than recommended nor add more water after the paste settles.

How to use?

Is specially designed for gypsum board treatment, combinated with union tape.
Before sealing the joints, not completely jointed boards must be refilled.
The product application uses to be manual, introducing the product between the joints with a little spatula and placing a union tape afterwards.
The product gives the chance of mechanic work.
Once the joint is filled, cover and plaster the product area, let dry and cover the tape.
Cover the screws' heads.
Apply the tape by the ceiling.
Use armed tape or metallic angular profile for out vertical angles (corners).
Joint paste can be used to repair little damages made in the board placing, as well as maintenance works.

Where to use?

Gypsum board rejointment, combinated with union tape.
Specially suitable for all kind of gypsum board with or without insulators, with lowered edges and cardboarded faces.
Do not use: On wet or frosted surfaces.

PAPRELASTIC

Crack covering elastic paste

What is this product?

Paprelastic is a special paste, ready to use, specially designed to fill and cover little lines and cracks, caused by gypsum and cement setting, avoiding its reappearance. It fills fissures and pores, leaving a uniform finish, with the possibililty of painting direcltly over.
Product also advised for surfaces of gypsum, cement, concrete, etc with possibility of fissures or microfissures, cracks or pores; being applicated in all surface, avoiding its posterior visual appearance thanks to its special characteristics of adaptability and flexibility.
Paprelastic is specially indicated for wall restoration, being able to cover tiles and painting directly over it. Also suitable for wet rooms like kitchens or bathrooms.
Adhesive product: It joins materials of a different nature or absortion ratio.
Product advised for filling expansion joints (Up to 2 mm).
Apply with trowel or spatula.

Characteristics

  • High filling capacity for cracks and joints until 2 mm.
  • Great adherence on many king of surfaces.
  • Thin coat, ready to paint in 3 hours.
  • High fungicide power.
  • Good workability.
  • Monocomponent.
  • Paintable.
  • Interior and exterior.
  • Impermeable
  • Elastic.
  • Water resistant.
  • Weatherproof.

Where to use?

Gypsum board rejointment, combinated with union tape.
Specially suitable for all kind of gypsum board with or without insulators, with lowered edges and cardboarded faces.
Do not use: On wet or frosted surfaces.
