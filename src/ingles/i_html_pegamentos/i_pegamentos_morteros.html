Mortero Seco de Proyección
Dry mortar for mechanical application

What is this product?

It's a grey mortar to be projected with machine on brick walls or concrete blocks, acting as a glue for the fiberglass boards or rockwool rolls.
Also for cement walls.

Characteristics

  • Easy application with projection machinery.
  • Great adherence power for aislant elements placing.
  • Waterproof: Protects against humidity.
  • Monocomponent.

How to prepare?

This product is specially designed to be applicated with projection machinery.
It's recommended to adjust the machine in a 25% mortar/water relation and modify this relation until getting the paste density wanted.
The mortar/water relation may change depending on working conditions.
In case of hand application, mix in a 6-7 water litres bowl per each 25 mortar kgs.

How to use?

Project the mortar on the wall.
The water volume must be graduated to get a paste density with no slipping-off.
Once projected, mortar must be smoothed with a trowel, trying that all the surface is covered and eliminating at once possible air bags.
Resultant coat width must be between 5 and 8 mm.
Fix the fiberglass or rockwool panels through manual pression.
It's recommended to place these aislant panels from up to down, for the panels that must be cutted are the ones down, in order to ease the work as much as possible.
Fix the panels during the "open time" (20 minutes).
Out of this time, its properties are reduced.

Mortero Seco
Dry mortar

What is this product?

It's a grey mortar ready to use mixed with water.
Its principal applications are given in little domestic use works for brick placing, walls, floors, etc...

Characteristics

  • Easy application.
  • Do-it-yourself indicated product.
  • Great commodity (cement and sand already mixed).
  • Great adherence power for aislant elements fixing.
  • Monocomponent (Just add water).

How to prepare?

  1. The mixing is traditional, like every mortar.
  2. Add the water it needs, to user's convenience until getting a strong and consistent (usually 4 litres per each 25 kgs).

How to use?

Ceramic material fixing, for interiors and exteriors.
Using substrates: Cement floors and walls, cement-lime mortar brick.
Substrates must be solid, clean, dry and without deformations.
Do not use: On metal, fiber-cement, wood and old tiles.
For these applications, mix PANZER with ADESILATEX.
Over concrete, mix MORTERO SECO with ADESILATEX.

Mortero Refractario
Refractory mortar

What is this product?

It’s a grey refractory mortar for wide coat, prepaired against fire and high temperatures.
It’s applied in the construction of elements where there will be high temperatures such as ovens, chimneys, barbacues, fireplaces...

Characteristics

  • Great resistance to high temperatures.
  • Great adherence power for laying refractory elements.
  • Monocomponent
  • Easy to use

How to prepare?

  1. Pour the water first and then, the refractory mortar.
  2. Mix in bowls in an approximated proportion of 4,5 litres per each 25 kgs
  3. It’s very important to not add more water than recommended because chemical and physical conditions of the product would be reduced.
  4. Hand mix or with a mixing machine (better slow, 500 rpm) until getting a creamy paste.
  5. Never add water after the paste settles.

How to use?

Once the paste is ready, it must be worked as a traditional mortar, for the following applications:

  • Union of rebellious bricks
  • Wide coat cement walls

It’s recommended to clean the pieces to join and moiste them if porousity is high.
It’s important that before the product forges, refractory bricks joints are touched up.

Where to use?

Ceramic material fixing, for interiors and exteriors.
Using substrates: Cement and cement-lime mortar walls Substrates must be solid, hard, clean and dry, without cracks or deformations.
Do not use: On metal, fiber-cement or wood (no flexible)

Papresa Shikkui 0,2
Ultrawhite flexible finish paste

What is this product?

It is a special calcium carbonate finish mortar, extra white, to apply on brick walls, concrete blocks, mortars. Leaves a fine white finish with a high resistance, endurance and flexibility.

Characteristics

  • Easy application by hand.
  • Extra whiteness.
  • Extra flexibility to avoid cracks on extreme conditions.
  • Hidrofuge: Protects against humidity.
  • Monocomponent.
  • Interior and exterior use.

How to prepare?

  1. This product is specially designed to be applicated by hand.
  2. Mix PAPRESA SHIKKUI 0,2 with 6-7 litres of clean water by each 25 kg bag.

How to use as a finish mortar?

  • Mix PAPRESA SHIKKUI 0,2 with water, better with slow mixing machine until getting a creamy paste.
  • Apply with trowel.
  • Resultant coat width must be between 1 and 6 mm.

How to use as a joint paste?

  • Mix PAPRESA SHIKKUI 0,2 with water, better with slow mixing machine until getting a creamy paste.
  • Apply with trowel or metal spatula.
  • It can be used together with mesh or cover tape.
